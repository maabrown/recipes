2 Pieces of Bread
1 Jar of Peanut Butter
1 Jar of Jelly

Dip your table knife into the jar of peanut butter. Spread the peanut
butter on one side of one of the pieces of bread. Rinse knife under
running water. Dip your knife into the jar of jelly. Spread the jelly
on one side of the second piece of bread. Place the side of bread with
peanut butter on top of the piece of bread with jelly on it. (You can
cut off the crust of the bread if you desire). Put in mouth and eat.
